Industrial robots are many different types and models designed to automate processes in industry. Here are a few basic types of industrial robots:

Robotic Manipulators: These are the most common type of industrial robots. They usually have many joints that allow them to implement and perform various tasks. Robotic arms are often used for welding, loading and unloading, assembling, and for performing complex operations on objects.

CNC robots: CNC robots are highly accurate and repeatable. They are used to perform operations that require a high degree of automation such as milling, drilling, engraving and 3D printing.

Welding Robots: These robots specialize in welding various materials including metals and plastics. They are usually equipped with special welding fixtures and can work at high speed with high precision.

Assembly robots: These robots are used to automatically assemble and assemble various components and devices. They can perform complex tasks with great speed and precision, making them ideal for electronics, automotive and other manufacturing applications.

Material Handling Robots: These robots are designed to process a variety of materials such as wood, metal, glass and plastic. They can be used for cutting, grinding, polishing and other surface finishing operations.
